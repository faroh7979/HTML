SPONSORSHIPS & PARTNERSHIPS

Support for Bulgarian sports is at the heart of the philosophy behind the WINBET brand. The company is a proud partner of the football teams of CSKA Sofia, Lokomotiv Plovdiv, Etar Veliko Tarnovo, Botev Vratsa, Tsarsko Selo, Botev Plovdiv, the volleyball teams of Levski, CSKA and Neftohimik Burgas, the basketball clubs of Levski and CSKA, as well as other teams and federations in various sports. WINBET is a partner of some of the leading Bulgarian athletes, such as the boxing brothers Kubrat and Tervel Pulevi. The company also supports the organization of major sporting events in Bulgaria, such as the ATP Garanti Koza Sofia Open 2017 tennis tournament and the Footballer of the Year Awards ceremony.
